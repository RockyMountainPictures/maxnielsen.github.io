A photoshoot of puppies,

I captured the important stages of development of poodle puppies for Lumiere Standard Poodles, a poodle breeder in Port Moody, BC.

I developed my skills from my previous commission in lighting and staging. I also learned how to shoot in unexpected conditions; in difficult environments and with rowdy subjects.
